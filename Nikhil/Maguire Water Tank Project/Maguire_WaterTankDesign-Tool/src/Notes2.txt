1. Anchor Bolt Module
---------------------
The Anchor Bolt module was engineered to handle high-precision layout and structural capacity checks for circular tank foundations.

A. Structural Logic & Formulas:
   - Tension Demand: Implemented the 'Circular Group' method (Tb = 2 * Tu / Nb), which is the most accurate for circular bolt patterns subjected to overturning moments.
   - Tensile & Shear Capacity: Aligned with ACI 318-19 and AISC. Includes Phi reduction factors (φ = 0.75 for tension/shear).
   - Interaction Check: Implemented the combined tension and shear interaction formula [(Tu/φPn) + (Vu/φVn) <= 1.0].
   - Concrete Breakout: Integrated kc-based breakout strength formulas for deep anchorages.

   - Validation: Integrated unit conversion logic to handle Kips vs. Lbs, preventing order-of-magnitude errors.

2. Base Plate Module
--------------------
The Base Plate module handles the structural design of annular sector plates, ensuring safe load transfer from the tank shell to the concrete foundation.

A. Geometric Accuracy (Sections 1.1 - 4.4):
   - Gross/Net Area: High-precision calculation of annular sectors, accounting for segment angles and bolt-hole deductions.
   - Centroid Radius (rBar): Implemented the complex centroidal distance formula for sectors: 
     rBar = [4*sin(θ/2) / 3*θ] * [(Ro³ - Ri³) / (Ro² - Ri²)]
     Verified against 'Base Plate Results.PNG' to 4 decimal places.

B. Structural Design (Sections 5.1 - 6.3):
   - Shell Radius Logic: A critical refinement that calculates the maximum cantilever length (l) as max(Ro - Rshell, Rshell - Ri). This accounts for shell offsets from the plate centerline.
   - Bearing Strength: Aligned with ACI 318-19 Section 22.8. Includes the sqrt(A2/A1) enhancement factor (capped at 2.0) with φ = 0.65.
   - Required Thickness (treq): Based on AISC Design Guide 1 (LRFD):
     treq = l * sqrt[ (2 * fp) / (0.9 * Fy) ]
   - Bending Moment (Mu): Calculates the factored moment demand at the critical section (face of the shell).

C. UI Layout & Professionalism:
   - 24 Result Fields: Provides a deep audit trail including Bearing Utilization, Thickness Utilization, Bending Mu, and Allowable Stress.
   - Unit Integration: Explicit labels for every field (ft, in, ksi, psi, %, sqft, cuft, kips).
